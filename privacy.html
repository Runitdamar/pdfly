Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Effective immediately

👋
Introduction

Welcome to Pdfly. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and PDF tools at pdfly-seven.vercel.app.

🔒 Your files are never stored on our servers. All processing happens instantly and files are deleted immediately after conversion.

📁
File Processing

When you use our PDF tools, here is exactly what happens to your files:

  • Files processed purely in your browser (Compress, Merge, Split, Image to PDF, PDF to JPG) — never leave your device.
  • Files sent to our conversion API (PDF to Word, Word to PDF) — are transmitted securely, processed instantly, and permanently deleted from the server immediately after conversion.
  • We do not read, analyze, store, or share the contents of your files under any circumstances.
📊
Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to operate and improve our service:

  • Usage data — pages visited, tools used, general browser/device type (via analytics).
  • Email address — only if you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter.
  • Local storage — we store a small usage counter in your browser to manage free conversion limits. This data never leaves your device.

We do not collect names, phone numbers, payment information, or any other personal data.

🍪
Cookies & Advertising

We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • Analytics — to understand how visitors use our site and improve our tools.
  • Advertising — we use Google AdSense to display ads. Google may use cookies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing history.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings. You can also disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

🤝
Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Pdfly:

  • iLoveAPI — handles PDF ↔ Word conversions. Files are processed under their privacy policy.
  • Google AdSense — displays advertisements on our site.
  • Formspree — handles newsletter subscriptions.
  • Vercel — hosts our website and serverless functions.

We carefully select third-party providers who maintain strong data security practices.

✉️
Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us through our Contact page.

We will respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 72 hours.
